Privacy Policy

At BioSense, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our gender and age detection service.

Information We Don't Collect

BioSense is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Image Storage: Images captured from your webcam are processed in real-time and are never stored on our servers.
  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for or collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information.
  • No Cookies: Our application does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to monitor your behavior.

How Processing Works

When you use BioSense:

  • Images from your webcam are sent to our server for processing only after you explicitly click the "Analyze Face" button.
  • Our AI algorithms detect faces, gender, and approximate age.
  • Results are sent back to your browser immediately.
  • The original image and all analysis data are deleted from our server memory as soon as processing is complete.

Data Security

All data transmission between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS protocols. Since we don't store your images or personal information, there is minimal risk of a data breach affecting your privacy.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any persistent data in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access our service.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@biosense-demo.com.
